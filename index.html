About Our Church

God's Light Restoration Ministry is brought to life to restore and lead lost souls to God. We are a welcoming congregation that believes in the power of faith, the importance of community, and the call to serve others.

Our mission is to create a loving environment where people can grow in their relationship with God, build meaningful connections with others, and discover their purpose in life.

Whether you're new to faith or have been on your spiritual journey for years, we invite you to join our church family.
